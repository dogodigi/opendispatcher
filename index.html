What is Opendispatcher?

Opendispatcher is a modern client server application that uses maps to show firemen information about locations and sites in operation. Opendispatcher evolved from the Dutch national standard for Digitale bereikbaarheidskaarten, a standard that helps firemen identify locations, important information for operating during an incident and hazards. Opendispatcher is a pure browserbased solution and harnesses a responsive design enabling the use on tablets and smartphones.

Opendispatchers implements EU-GHS and NEN1414 symbology to display information on the map.
